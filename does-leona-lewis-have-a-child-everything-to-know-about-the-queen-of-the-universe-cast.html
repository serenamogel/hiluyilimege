Does Leona Lewis Have A Child? Everything To Know About The Queen Of The Universe Cast

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, May 29, 2024

Leona Lewis is revealed as one of the judges in the new show Queen of the Universe. Does she have a child? Learn everything about the British singer.

Leona Louise Lewis, a singer, songwriter, model, and actress from England, is better known as Leona Lewis. She rose in popularity after she won the 2006 X-Factor UK.

Leona also received a million-pound record deal with Syco Music. Her debut studio album Spirit was released in 2007. 

Her album was also the 4th best-selling album in the 2000s.

In 2014, She entered the film industry with her role in Walking on Sunshine. After two years, in 2016, she made her debut in broadway theatre. She is a 6-time BRIT Award and three-time Grammy Award, nominee. The singer is vegan and is involved in various charities for animal rights.

Leona was a recurring cast member in the Tv series The Oath. Currently, she is in the news as she will be appearing as one of the five judges in paramount’s upcoming drag-singing reality show “Queen Of The Universe.”

Does Leona Lewis Have A Child?

Leona Lewis does not have any children. However, she has spoken about adopting children in the past. She stated she wanted to adopt in an interview because her mother grew up in a children’s home.

She has publicly stated that she is eager to have kids. After some discussion with her husband, the pair decided to adopt instead.

Does Leona Lewis Have A Husband?

Leona Lewis is married to her husband, Dennis Jauch. He is a professional dancer and choreographer. The two were long-term fiances and got hitched in July 2019.

Leona and Dennis had been dating since 2010. They got married in the Palagio estate, Tuscany, Italy. The pair are rumored to be living in their $2.6 million Studio City home, which was purchased in March 2021.

What Is Leona Lewis Net Worth?

Leona Lewis has a net worth of over $10 million, according to Wealthy Gorilla’s website. Leona has multiple sources of income like acting, judging, and brand endorsements.

But, her primary source of income is made from record sales. As of 2021, She has sold more than 35 million records globally, according to her record label.

What Is Leona Lewis Instagram?

Leona Lewis is on Instagram under the username @leonalewis. She has a following of 702,000 followers on her account. Right now, she has 33,66 posts on her feed.

The reality singing show judge is verified on the platform and mostly posts elegant and aesthetic pictures. Recently she posted about her upcoming 2022 winter tour in the UK.
