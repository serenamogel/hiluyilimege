Shop Emma Stones Oscars Beauty Products 2024

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, February 19, 2024

Emma Stone, beauty-obsessed Hollywood starlets like Jodie Turner-Smith, and TikTokers alike can’t get enough of this now-iconic beauty product. Stone sported the color 2 Fair, while Turner-Smith went with 8 Deep.

Many brands have tried to dupe this product, but I gotta be real: there’s simply no comparison. Or as Naomi Campbell once said on The Face, “Don't compare yourself to me, ever. You’re not on my level.”

Hollywood Flawless Filter’s genius is that it’s a multifunctional product that can be applied all over underneath the foundation as a face primer, on its own, or just on the high points of the face for a major glow.

As someone with oily AF skin, I even enjoy using this product during the late summer, fall, and winter seasons for a gorgeous, non-greasy glow. It doesn’t cause breakouts, or look chalky on my skin. It’s a must-have product for anyone looking to achieve the most envy-inducing glow possible.
