About

2017 Rock and Roll Hall of Fame inductee as a member of the band Journey whose 3 stints with the group include 1978 to 1985, 1995 to 1998 and 2015 and on.

Before Fame

He was the recipient of his first drum kit when he was just 2 years old, and began taking lessons at age 9. He played with a group called Lin Biviano Big Band for 2 years beginning at the age of 19. He attended Berklee College of Music.

Trivia

He was named the #1 All-Around Drummer five years consecutively by Modern Drummer magazine readers. He was induced into the the Modern Drummer Hall of Fame in 2002.
