Rabbi David Ellenson Died: Know More About Him, Cause of Death?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, February 3, 2024

Rabbi David Ellenson, a cherished member of the Center for Small Town Jewish Life advisory board, sadly passed away on Thursday, December 7, 2023, in his home in New York. His life and contributions left an indelible mark on the Jewish world.

Who was Rabbi David Ellenson?

Rabbi David Ellenson hailed from Newport News, Virginia, and has carried the warmth of small-town life with him throughout his remarkable career. A distinguished scholar, Rabbi Ellenson played a significant role in inspiring and educating thousands in the Jewish community. Beyond his professional achievements, he was a beloved mentor, impacting individuals from their undergraduate years to their rabbinate. Rabbi Ellenson’s character was defined by love, wisdom, and generosity, making him a unique treasure in the hearts of those who knew him.
