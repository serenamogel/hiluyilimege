Lil Durk Biography, Age, Career, Net Worth, Height, Girlfriend

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, April 4, 2024

Lil Durk is an American rapper and singer from Chicago, Illinois known for being the lead member and founder of the Only The Family (OFT). His debut album, “Rember My Name” was released in 2015. His appearance on the Billboard Hot 100 was his single track “Viral Moment”. Some of his top songs include “3 Headed Goat”, “Backdoor” and “The Voice”. In this post, we will look at Lil Durk’s Biography, Age, Career, Net Worth, Height, Girlfriend and other facts surrounding his career. Keep reading.

Lil Durk Biography

Durk was born on 19 October 1992 in Englewood on the south side of Chicago, Illinois. Growing up was tough for him. His father was imprisoned when he was just 7 months old. Lil Durk recalled that at times when there was no food to eat.

Despite the struggles, Lil Durk had a chance to gain recognition through social apps like Myspace and YouTube. He gained more followers as a result of his raps.  Lil Durk couldn’t finish school at Paul Robeson High School. He dropped out of high school and joined a street gang based in Chicago called the “Black Disciples”. He had a problem with the law for joining the gang.

In 2011, Lil Durk took his music career seriously. He started as a full-time rapper and released two singles dubbed “Sneak Dissin'” and “I’ma Hitta”. These two singles receive good feedback so on 19 October 2012, he dropped his third mixtape titled “Life Ain’t No Joke”. The same year in December, he released another track called “L’s Anthem” which featured French Montana. In the list below, let us look at some of his albums and songs.
