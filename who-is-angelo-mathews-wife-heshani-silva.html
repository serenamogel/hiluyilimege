Heshani Silva: Wife Of Angelo Mathews Explored

In Sri Lanka, she competed in under-19 badminton tournaments. Heshani was ranked eighth in national badminton, according to some statistics.

According to reports, Heshani’s father is Prasanna Silva, who owns a construction company. Shyama Silva is Heshani’s mother, and her older sister Prashant and younger sister Shenali Silva are her siblings.
