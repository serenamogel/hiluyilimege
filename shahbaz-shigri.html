Shahbaz Shirgri was born on 24 February 1988 in Islamabad, Pakistan. He is a multi-talented actor as well as producer, cinematographer, and director. He received his education from Islamabad and has done specialization in film making from New York Film Academy. He is best known for Slackistan 2010 with co-stars Aisha Linnea Akthar, Rafey Alam, and Osman Khalid Butt.

Career Journey

As an actor, Shahbaz Shigri made his acting debut with Slackistan in 2010. The movie could not earn good but he got succeed in stepping up into the film industry. Shahbaz is so passionate about his work and this led him to be a film director. In 2012, he directed a Pakistani comedy movie named ‘Gol Chakkar’. The cast includes Ali Rehman Khan, Uzair Jaswal, and Usman Mukhtar. In 2017, Shahbaz Shigri also worked as an assistant director for Verna starring Mahira Khan and Haroon Shahid. As a cinematographer, he worked hard for the sarcastic thriller film ‘Anima State’ directed by Hammad Khan, a London-based director.

As a producer, In 2019, Shahbaz Shigri made his debut by producing the Pakistani romantic comedy film ‘Parey Hut Love’ featuring talented stars like Shehryar Munawar, Maya Ali, and Zara Noor Abbas. This movie was the greatest hit of 2019.

Personal Life

Shahbaz Shigri tied the knot with Aisha Linnea in 2014 but the couple divorced after 5 years of marriage. This news left their fans shockingly devastated because they were the cutest and the most loved-up couple in town. Shahbaz Shigri and Aisha Linnea got a divorce in 2018.

In 2021, Shahbaz Shigri and Pakistani singer Aima Baig officially announced their engagement. They are set to be married by the end of this year
