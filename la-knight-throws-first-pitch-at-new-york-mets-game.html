LA Knight Throws First Pitch At New York Mets Game

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, February 12, 2024

LA Knight is taking over in terms of popularity in WWE, and there is a lot of momentum behind him. As fans wait to yell “YEAH” back at LA Knight, fans at the baseball game got to see a little bit more of the former Max Dupri.

LA Knight received the honor of throwing out the first pitch at the New York Mets game today. It was quite a treat for fans at the ballpark, because they might not have realized they were getting to see LA Knight as well.

Fans at the ballpark yelled “YEAH” at LA Knight, and you can hear that in the video below. It seems that LA Knight had a great time representing WWE today in a much different venue.

WrestleMania is taking over Philly, and the company doesn’t want anyone to forget it. Obviously, LA Knight will represent WWE in the process, and hopefully he will get a spot on the show of shows as well. They were quick to remind fans in NYC that Philly is getting Mania next year.

Only time will tell what is next for LA Knight. He had quite a good time today, and odds are those engagements where he puts his face in front of WWE to represent the company won’t come to an end any time soon.

LA Knight worked hard throughout his entire career, grinding, and doing what he can to get attention. Now that he’s finally doing his thing, and getting a nice push, in WWE, there doesn’t seem to be any limit to what he can do next.
