Bad Bunny Albums: How Many Albums Have Bad Bunny Released?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, March 2, 2024

Bad Bunny Albums: How Many Albums Have Bad Bunny Released?: Bad Bunny, officially known as Benito Antonio Martínez Ocasio is a Puerto Rican rapper and singer born on March 10, 1994.

He developed a love for music at a very younger age but started to write and create his own interpretations at the age of 14 until, in 2013, he began to publish his songs through SoundCloud.

After releasing a couple of songs which include; Get, Tentacion, and Just Let Me Know, among others, he rose to prominence in 2016 with his song ‘Diles’, which led to a deal with Hear This Music owned by DJ Luian.

During the subsequent years, he continued gaining traction with songs such as Soy Peor as well as collaborations with Farruko, Karol G, Ozuna, J Balvin, and more.

Bad Bunny had his major breakthrough in 2018 when he bagged a feature on Cardi B’s number-one song ‘I Like It’ alongside J Balvin and his top-ten song ‘Mia’ featuring Drake.

He has risen through the ranks to become one of the most sought-after acts and is credited with helping Spanish-language music achieve mainstream popularity in the worldwide market.

In 2020, he became the first non-English language act to be Spotify’s most streamed artist of the year and achieved the same record again in 2021. He also had the biggest streaming year for any artist on Spotify in 2022.

Bad Bunny has earned two Grammy Awards, four Latin Grammy Awards, eight Billboard Music Awards, and thirteen Premios lo Nuestro awards. He won the Artist of the Year at the Apple Music Awards 2022.

His musical style is primarily defined as Latin trap and reggaeton, although his music also takes influences from other genres. In February 2023, he made headlines after delivering a stellar performance at the 2023 GRAMMYs.

The Puerto Rican mega-star performed songs from his latest studio album ‘Un Verano Sin Ti’ which made history as the first Spanish-language album nominated for Album Of The Year.

Bad Bunny Albums: How Many Albums Have Bad Bunny Released?

Bad Bunny has served the music community with several singles and four solo studio albums. He also has one collaborative studio album with J Balvin, a Colombian rapper, singer, and record producer.

Solo Studio Albums

  • X 100pre (2018)
  • YHLQMDLG (2020)
  • El Último Tour Del Mundo (2020)
  • Un Verano Sin Ti (2022)

Collaborative Studio Albums

  • Oasis (with J Balvin) (2019)
