Jessica Simpson Children: Meet Her 3 Children

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, March 15, 2024

Jessica Simpson Children: Meet Her 3 Children: Jessica Simpson, officially known as Jessica Ann Simpson is an American singer, actress, entrepreneur, and philanthropist.

She developed a love for music at a younger age and has been consistent throughout her career rising through the ranks to become one of the most sought-after acts in the entertainment space.

After performing in church choirs as a child, she signed with Columbia Records in 1997, aged 17, and released her first studio album, Sweet Kisses in 1999 which she sold over two million copies in the United States.

As an actress, she has starred in several movies and is best known for her role in the romantic comedy film Employee Of The Month, and The Price of Beauty, among others.

Apart from her career as a singer and an actress, Jessica launched The Jessica Simpson Collection in 2005, a fashion-focused line with 34 product categories to date, the brand has earned over $1 billion in revenue and is the most successful celebrity licensing the brand in history.

In February 2023, she made major headlines when she detailed a secret romance she had with a “massive movie star” years ago.

Jessica Simpson tells all in a new short story she’s written for Amazon Original Stories, Movie Star, out Wednesday, February 1, 2023. She revealed a lot about their romance, how they met, where they kissed for the first time, and how things ended.

But in all the revelations, she didn’t reveal the mystery man’s name. Jessica is keeping the actor’s name to herself for now and teases people: “I will tell you this… he is still a movie star!”

Jessica Simpson Children: Meet Her 3 Children

Jessica Simpson has been blessed with three children; Maxwell Drew Johnson (born in 2012), Ace Knute Johnson (born in 2013), and Birdie Mae Johnson (2019).
