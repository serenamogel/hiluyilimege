No Stir Almond Butter and More

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, October 4, 2024

The best almond butter is packed with almond flavor, it’s not too oily, and it’s great for myriad culinary uses.

I rarely think about almond butter, so I had to do my research before heading into our almond butter taste tests. In my mind, most people eat almond butter because they think it’s a “healthy” alternative to peanut butter. Then, there are some people who opt for almond butter because they are allergic to peanuts. That’s it. Those are the two people that eat almond butter—or so I thought. After tasting my way through more than a dozen brands, flavors, and styles of almond butter over the course of two separate taste tests, I’m singing a different tune. Almond butter—whether it’s no stir almond butter, crunchy almond butter, or creamy almond butter—is its own unique thing that deserves respect.

We decided that we were looking for a pleasant texture and a notable almond flavor. What we didn’t want to see was that thick, oily layer at the top of the jar. Here’s the best almond butter you can buy.

Best Crunchy

Maisie Jane’s Crunchy Almond ButterSproutsWhole Foods

I rarely see almond butters that aren’t smooth, so I was intrigued by Maisie Jane’s Crunchy Almond Butter. It’s speckled with tiny chunks of nuts, but this nut butter still maintains a smooth, creamy base that perfectly contrasts with the almond pieces. It has a complex, deep flavor because it’s made with 100% dry roasted almonds. The natural, unprocessed texture and the cute little bee on the jar make me feel effortlessly outdoorsy and in touch with nature (like someone who lives a #VanLife). This is the best almond butter if you like a little crunch.

Credit: Liv Averett / Whole Foods

8/10

Best for Almond Lovers

Trader Joe’s Creamy Almond Butter No Salt

I was shocked when I realized that Trader Joe’s Creamy Almond Butter contains no added sugar or salt. It manages to be lightly sweetened and a little salty without any help. Since it’s protein-packed and totally sugarless, this is the best almond butter for breakfast. Stir it into oatmeal, add a scoop to Greek yogurt, or smear some onto a toaster waffle with some strawberries. Don’t get me wrong, though. This would also be good in homemade chocolate almond butter cups or coconut almond butter milkshakes.

Credit: Liv Averett / Amazon

Rating:

8.5/10

Best Flavored

RXBAR RX Nut Butter Vanilla Almond ButterTarget

RXBAR RX Vanilla Almond Butter tastes exactly like Nilla Wafers. What more could you want from a health food product? The smell reminds me of birthday cake lipgloss. Does it look a little gross when you open the jar? Yes. Why does almond butter need eggs in it? I have no clue. What I do know is that this stuff is tasty. If you’re in the market for a protein almond butter for snacks or smoothies, this is the best almond butter you can buy.

Credit: Liv Averett / Target

Rating:

9/10

Best for Sandwiches

Maranatha No Stir Creamy Almond ButterTarget

Maranatha No Stir Creamy Almond Butter is thicker than most of the almond butters we tried, making it perfect for sandwiches. This plus banana plus honey? A dream come true. The flavor is extremely nutty without blasting you with almond, which happened with some of the other butters we tried. If you like eco-friendly foods, Maranatha uses ethically sourced palm oil, is packaged in jars made from recycled glass, and is a “proud supporter” of the bee conservation efforts of the Xerces Society.

Credit: Liv Averett / Target

Rating:

9.5/10

Best Roasted

Sam’s Choice Slow Roasted Creamy Almond ButterWalmart

Sam’s Choice Creamy Almond butter is thick, luscious, sweet almond butter perfection. The nuts taste toasted without tasting bitter or burnt like other nut butters I’ve tasted. It’s really pleasantly sweet without being cloying. A hint of salt finishes each bite. Bonus: There’s not an upsetting amount of oil when you open the jar. I typically wouldn’t choose almond butter over peanut butter, but I would take Sam’s Choice Creamy Almond Butter over any other nut butter on the market.

Credit: Ryan Martin / Walmart

Rating:

9.5/10

Best Chocolate

Good & Gather Dark Chocolate Creamy Almond ButterTarget

This flavored almond butter promises dark chocolate, and it gives you dark chocolate. That is why it is one of the best almond butter options at the grocery store. The chocolate flavor is rich and dark without being bitter. I would love to eat an apple dipped in this. The fresh crunch and the luxurious chocolate would be a match made in heaven.

Best Multi-Purpose

Maranatha Coconut Almond ButterWalmart

Maranatha Coconut Almond Butter is so unbelievably good. It’s creamy, no stir, and tastes like coconut without tasting like a candle. This is one of the best flavored almond butter brands we tried and it’s one of the best almond butter options in general. I specifically named it the best multi-purpose almond butter because this would be great on its own, incorporated into desserts, and as an ingredient in savory dishes. I want to make chicken satay with this stuff so bad.

Best Flavored

Good & Gather Cinnamon Almond ButterTarget

This is, hands down, the best flavored almond butter we tried. It has the perfect amount of cinnamon. It’s not spicy and it doesn’t taste like fall or a cinnamon roll. It tastes like great almond butter with a touch of cinnamon. It’s pleasantly salty. Despite this being flavored, it wouldn’t clash with jelly in an almond butter and jelly sandwich. Honestly, no notes.

Best of the Best

Good & Gather Creamy No Stir Almond ButterTarget

Good & Gather Creamy No Stir Almond Butter is such a treat. It’s so good that it’s even better than all the fun, flavored almond butter. All the almond butters on this list are really good, and this one still managed to be the best almond butter of all. It’s so simple, but it is everything you want in an almond butter. It’s a tad sweet, it’s nice and salty, there’s plenty of toasted almond flavor, it’s creamy, and it’s no stir. If you haven’t been buying almond butter at Target, you should really start.
