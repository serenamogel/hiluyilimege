Pete Wentz Biography/ Wiki

Pete Wentz was born on June 5, 1979, as Peter Lewis Kingston Wentz III. He is an American musician, singer, songwriter, A&R consultant, and record executive. Pete is best known as the bassist for the American rock band Fall Out Boy.

Pete Wentz Age and Birthday

Wentz is 40 years old as of 2019, he was born on June 5, 1979, in Wilmette, Illinois, United States of America. He celebrates his birthday on June 5th every year. Pete will be 41 years old on June 5th, 2020.

Pete Wentz Height

Wentz stands at 5 feet 6 inches (1.68 m).

Pete Wentz Education

Wentz attended New Trier High School and North Shore Country Day School, where he was an all-state soccer player. During his first year of high school, he started missing school frequently and smoking weed with mates but later quit as it was affecting his grades at school. After graduating from high school in 1997, he joined DePaul University, where he studied political science. But he later quit school to pursue music with his band Fall Out Boy.

Pete Wentz Family

Peter Lewis Kingston Wentz III was born in Wilmette, Illinois, a well-to-do suburb of Chicago. His parents are Dale Wentz, a high school admissions counselor, and Pete Wentz II, an attorney. Wentz is of English and German origin on his father’s side and Afro-Jamaican descent on his mother’s side. He has two siblings, a younger sister named Hillary, and a younger brother known as Andrew. His grandfather, Arthur Winston Lewis, served as the U.S. Ambassador to Sierra Leone; Arthur Winston Lewis’ cousin is General Colin Powell.

Pete Wentz Wife | Kids | Girlfriend

In 2006, Wentz started dating singer Ashlee Simpson. In April 2008, Simpson and Wentz confirmed their engagement and were married on May 17, 2008, at Simpson’s parents’ residence in Encino, California. Simpson gave birth to their son, Bronx Mowgli, on November 20, 2008. Things did not last and On February 8, 2011, Simpson filed for divorce, citing “irreconcilable differences”. Their divorce was finalized on November 22, 2011.

Peter is currently dating American model Megan Camper. On February 17, 2014, Wentz and his current girlfriend, Meagan Camper announced that the couple was expecting their first child together and their son Saint Lazslo Wentz was born in August 2014. On January 2, 2018, Wentz posted to his Instagram that he and Megan were expecting their second child together. Their daughter Marvel Jane Wentz was born on May 13, 2018.
