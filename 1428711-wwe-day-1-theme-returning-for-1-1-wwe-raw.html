WWE Day 1 Theme Returning For 1/1 WWE RAW

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, July 11, 2024

WWE is bringing back the WWE Day 1 theme.

WWE held Day 1 as a premium live event on January 1, 2022. Another show under the same name was scheduled for 2023, but it was reportedly canceled. The theme is returning in 2024, but it won’t be for a Premium Live Event.

On the December 11 episode of WWE RAW, general manager Adam Pearce announced that Drew McIntyre would challenge Seth Rollins for the WWE World Heavyweight Championship on the January 1 episode of WWE RAW. The show was called to the “Day 1” edition of Monday Night RAW, which will be held in San Diego, California, on January 1.

WrestleZone will provide more information as it becomes available.

What Happened At WWE Day 1 2022?

To this day, WWE Day 1 2022 remains a memorable show. Brock Lesnar was set to face Universal Champion Roman Reigns, but the match was called off when Reigns tested positive for COVID-19. Lesnar was then added to the WWE Championship match. He ultimately beat Big E, Seth Rollins, Bobby Lashley, and Kevin Owens to win the title.

Elsewhere on the show, Becky Lynch defeated Liv Morgan to retain the RAW Women’s Championship, and Edge beat The Miz. Additionally, The Usos defeated The New Day to retain the SmackDown Tag Team Championship.
