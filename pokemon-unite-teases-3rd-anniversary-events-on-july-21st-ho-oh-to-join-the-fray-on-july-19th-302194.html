Pokmon UNITE teases 3rd Anniversary Events on July 21st Ho-Oh to join the fray on July 19th

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, January 25, 2024
Pokémon UNITE will be celebrating its third anniversary on July 21st. Ahead of this anniversary, the official Twitter accounts for the game have teased upcoming celebratory events with various key visuals, along with Ho-Oh's addition to the game on July 19th. The key visual also teases new Holowear for Charizard, Delphox, Ceruledge, Talonflame, and Chandelure. Additionally, new Noble Style: Mimikyu Holowear is also available starting June 27th.

Ho-Oh joins the fray
Ho-Oh will be the latest Pokémon added to the game, debuting July 19th. The announcement on the official website teases that Ho-Oh will be a Defender. While no further details have been revealed at this time, it can be expected that Ho-Oh will be classified as an EX license similar to Zacian and Mewtwo. As is usual for a new Pokémon debut, players can also look forward to a new Holowear released alongside the Unite License.
Update: Ho-Oh will be available in the public test server from July 1st. The Public Test app aims to gather data for balance changes and bug fixes, which will be utilized in future updates. The app is available only on Android devices in English in Japan, the Philippines, Indonesia, Thailand, Malaysia, Singapore, or India.

New Holowear for Mimikyu
Noble Style Holowear for Mimikyu will be available in the game starting June 27th. Players can get the Holowear for 2,199 Aeos Gems, or get a special bundle with Mimikyu's Unite License and Noble Style Holowear at a discounted price of 2,358 Aeos Gems instead of the full price of 2,774 Aeos Gems.

​


July's Unite Club membership bonus is "Fantasy Style: Scizor"
The reward for July's Unite Club membership will be Fantasy Style holowear for Scizor. With the monthly Unite Club membership, players receive new Holowear every month, and two trial Unite licenses and trial Holowear per week. Other benefits include 40 Aeos Gems per day, a discount on limited-time fashion items, and special members-only portrait frames and chat speech. bubbles.

Pika Party game mode continues with chance to win Holowear by completing missions
The Pika Party Quick Battle game mode, which was first introduced to the game for Pokémon UNITE's first anniversary, was made available again in the game from last weekend, running through to July 18th. In this mode, all Pokémon on the field—both wild and player-controlled—will be Pikachu! Additionally, players also have the chance to win Pikachu's Unite Style Holowear by completing missions. Various other Holowear for Pikachu are also on sale in the in-game shop.
