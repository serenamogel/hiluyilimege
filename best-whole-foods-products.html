Best Whole Foods Products: 18 Best 365 Brand Foods

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, June 22, 2024

What are the best Whole Foods products? After tasting many, many, many 365 brand products over the course of our many, many, many taste tests, we can tell you that the best things to buy at Whole Foods are condiments. Seriously, it turns out that Whole Foods makes great sauces and dips! Of course, that’s not all—but that is most of it. We scoured our rankings to find the 365 brand products that ended up in the number one or two spots. A lot of them were, indeed, condiments, but there are a few wild card products. The next time you’re wondering what to buy at Whole Foods, consult this list. Because these are the best items at Whole Foods.

Cocktail Sauce

Whole Foods 365 Organic Cocktail SauceAmazon

Making your own DIY shrimp cocktail? Pick up a bottle of this 365 brand cocktail sauce. There’s just enough grated horseradish to give it some zest and texture. And it’s pretty darn affordable at just over $3. Keep a bottle in the side door of your fridge and reach for it whenever fried seafood is on the menu.

Plant-Based Bolognese

Whole Foods 365 Plant-Based BologneseAmazon

This 365 brand plant-based bolognese gets its meatiness from portobello mushrooms. And while it’s not a true dupe for actual meat-based bolognese, it captures the essence. It’s strongly savory with a deep umami flavor that’s hard to achieve without meat. It’s one of the best things to buy at Whole Foods if you eat a plant-based diet or are just looking for ways to sneak in more veggies.
