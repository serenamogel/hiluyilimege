Collision on Route 125

A serious collision occurred on Route 125, near Glick Road, resulting in significant damage to two vehicles. The incident took place on December 13, at approximately 7:30 in the morning. Illinois State Police Troop 6 promptly responded to the scene to investigate the collision and ensure the safety of those involved.

Identification of Victim

The victim of the collision has been identified as a woman from Morgan County. Crystal Shanks, a 48-year-old resident of Literberry, tragically lost her life in the accident. Sangamon County Coroner Jim Allmon confirmed her passing, stating that she was pronounced dead at the scene. The authorities have conducted an autopsy, which revealed that Mrs. Shanks succumbed to severe blunt force injuries sustained during the collision.

Details of the Collision

According to officials from the State Police, the collision involved a Chevrolet pickup truck and a Kia SUV. The pickup truck was traveling west on Route 125, while the Kia SUV was heading east. The collision occurred when the Kia SUV attempted to pass another vehicle and veered into the westbound lane, colliding with the pickup truck. The impact of the collision caused significant damage to both vehicles.

Victim’s Fatality

The collision resulted in the tragic loss of Crystal Shanks’ life. Her passing is a devastating outcome of the accident, highlighting the importance of safe driving practices and vigilance on the road. Our thoughts and condolences go out to her family and loved ones during this difficult time.

Injuries of Pickup Truck Driver

The driver of the pickup truck sustained injuries in the collision. Although the specific nature of the injuries has not been disclosed, the driver was promptly taken to a nearby hospital for medical treatment. We hope for their swift recovery and return to good health.

Second Collision

In an unfortunate turn of events, a second collision occurred in close proximity to the initial accident site. This collision involved a passenger vehicle and a commercial motor vehicle. Fortunately, there were no reports of any injuries resulting from this second collision. The authorities swiftly responded to ensure the safety of all individuals involved and to prevent any further incidents.

Route 125 Traffic Restrictions

Following the collision, Route 125 experienced temporary traffic restrictions in both directions. These measures were put in place to facilitate the investigation and ensure the safety of motorists in the area. However, the road was reopened later the same day, allowing traffic to resume its normal flow. We appreciate the patience and understanding of the community during this necessary disruption.
