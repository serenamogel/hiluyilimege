The Meaning Behind The Song: Post Nut Clarity by Alemeda

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, April 25, 2024
TitlePost Nut Clarity
ArtistAlemeda
Writer/ComposerAlemeda, Becca Krueger
AlbumA1*
Release DateFebruary 17, 2022
GenrePop, Indie
ProducerAlemeda, Jack Kolbe, Henry Was

Post Nut Clarity, a song by Alemeda, explores the emotions and thoughts that often follow sexual encounters. The lyrics delve into the moments of self-reflection and introspection that can occur after the intensity of a sexual experience has passed. It touches upon themes of regret, miscommunication, and the importance of clear communication and consent.

The song opens with the narrator reminiscing about a sexual encounter. They recall being in bed with their partner, in a place called Sunset, with desires clouding their judgment. However, it becomes evident that the narrator feels some level of frustration and disappointment with the encounter. The lines “You didn’t even ask me what I wanted, You made dumb assumptions” suggest a lack of communication and understanding between the two individuals involved.

The chorus emphasizes the narrator’s frustration further, accusing their partner of being a liar and expressing the need to set the record straight. The repetition of the phrase “Clearly not your forte, Clearly don’t be fucking” highlights the importance of open communication and consent in intimate relationships.

The bridge repeats the same sentiments, emphasizing the narrator’s regret for not maintaining celibacy and allowing their partner into their bed without fully understanding their desires and intentions.

Alemeda’s lyrics capture a relatable theme that many people have experienced in their own lives. Post Nut Clarity explores the moments when one’s mind clears after intense physical intimacy and reveals the reality of the situation. It serves as a reminder to prioritize open communication, consent, and understanding in all intimate encounters.

I personally connect with this song on a deep level. It reminds me of moments in my own life when I have experienced post-sexual clarity. There have been instances where I allowed myself to be carried away by desire without considering my own wants and needs. The aftermath often left me feeling regretful and questioning my own decisions.

This song is an anthem for communication and consent. It serves as a reminder that sexual encounters should be based on mutual understanding and respect. The lyrics encourage individuals to engage in open conversations about desires, boundaries, and expectations before engaging in any physical intimacy.

As for Alemeda’s artistry, the song showcases their talent as both a songwriter and vocalist. The emotions conveyed through the lyrics are amplified by Alemeda’s expressive performance. The production by Alemeda, Jack Kolbe, and Henry Was complements the lyrics, creating an atmospheric and introspective ambiance.

In conclusion, Post Nut Clarity by Alemeda is a thought-provoking song that delves into the emotions and revelations that can follow moments of intense physical intimacy. It serves as a reminder of the importance of communication, consent, and understanding in all intimate encounters. So, let this song be a reminder to prioritize open conversations and clarity in your own relationships.
